Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the [Extension Name] Chrome extension (hereinafter referred to as the "Extension"). We take your privacy seriously and are committed to not collecting, storing, or uploading any of your personal data or browsing data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

This Extension does not collect, store, or upload any of the following data:

  • Your personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, etc.).
  • Your browsing history or website visit data.
  • Any content you enter on web pages.

All functionalities of the Extension run locally in your browser, and no data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Extension Functionality

This Extension provides the following functionalities:

  • Display original page image library and provide copy button functionality: The Extension reads the image library on the current page and provides a copy button for you to quickly copy image links or resources. This operation is performed locally in your browser, and no data is uploaded.
  • Directly insert or extract HTML content in the draft editor via the official page API: The Extension interacts with the official page API to support inserting or extracting HTML content in the draft editor. This operation is performed locally in your browser, and no data is uploaded.
  • Display the original page's followed user list and provide a button to copy the fakeid attribute of page elements: The Extension reads the followed user list on the current page and provides a button to copy the fakeid. This operation is performed locally in your browser, and no data is uploaded.

4. Permission Explanation

To achieve the above functionalities, the Extension requires the following permissions:

  • Access to specific websites: The Extension needs access to specific websites you use to read page content and provide related functionalities. The Extension will not access websites unrelated to its functionalities.
  • Read and modify page content: The Extension needs to read page content to display the image library, followed user list, etc., and may modify page content to support draft editor operations.
All permissions are used solely to implement the Extension's functionalities and will not be used for any other purposes.

5. Data Security

Since the Extension does not collect or upload any data, there is no risk of data storage or transmission. All functionalities run locally in your browser, ensuring your privacy is protected to the greatest extent.
